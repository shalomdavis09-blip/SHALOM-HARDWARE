About Shalom Hardware

Our Story

Shalom Hardware is a proud father-son business dedicated to providing high-quality hardware tools and materials to customers across Kenya and the world. With over a decade of experience, we've built our reputation on reliability, quality, and exceptional customer service.

Our Mission

To make quality hardware accessible to everyone, whether you're a professional contractor, DIY enthusiast, or home builder. We believe in fair pricing, honest dealings, and going the extra mile for our customers.
